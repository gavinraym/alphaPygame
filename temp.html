BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in Seoul in 2013. The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.

The group's music style includes a wide range of genres, such as hip hop, pop, and R&B. BTS has become one of the biggest and most successful boy bands in the world, with millions of fans around the globe.
